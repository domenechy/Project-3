The first step in receiving any type of federal financial aid is to complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). Federal student aid includes grants, work-study opportunities and student loans. The FAFSA is also required for some scholarships. Remember, you'll need to submit the FAFSA each year of college in order to stay eligible for federal financial aid.
2- The first letter in FAFSA stands for FREE. Never pay to complete the application.
