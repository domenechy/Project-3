Going to community college for the first 2 years is a smart decision and nothing to be ashamed of. It will save you a lot of money and the classes
offered during freshman and sophomore years are core courses, meaning they are mandatory for every major so essentially during the first 2 years
of college everyone is taking the same classes.Therefore universities will accept most if not all of your credits from community colleges.

According to best.colleges.com, these are the best 4 community colleges in New Jersey:

#1 Warren County Community College #2 Raritan Valley Community College
#3 Brookdale Community College #4 Union County College

***There are plenty more communnity colleges with a good reputation. Do a Google search to find the closest ones around you***
